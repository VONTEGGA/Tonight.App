# Classic Beef Burger Patty

## Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder

## Cooking Instructions:
1. Preheat the grill.
2. Form the beef into patties.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
4. Grill for 4-5 minutes per side.

## Prep/Cook Times:
- Prep Time: 10 minutes
- Cook Time: 10 minutes

## Difficulty Level:
- Easy

## Food Pairing Recommendations:
- French fries, coleslaw.
